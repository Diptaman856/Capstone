What is Coding....?

Coding is another term for programming, and it refers to the process of writing instructions for a computer to follow in order to perform specific tasks. In essence, coding is the act of writing the "Code" or the set of commands using programming language.

.....While programming is the broader concept that involves problem-solving, algorithm design, and software architecture, coding specifically refers to the act of translating those solutions and designs into machine-readable instructions, often in the form of text.
